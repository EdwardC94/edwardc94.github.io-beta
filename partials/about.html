About

My Name

Let me introduce myself beginning with an explanation of the origin of my name. Before I was even born my mother decided she would christen me as "Eduardo" due to one of the meanings of that name, "wealthy guardian". That to me is a constant reminder of the desire of my mother to see me build my own wealth and of protecting our greatest treasure, my sister.

My Passion

The one thing I enjoy the most in life is learning. With web-development I realized that whatever we think we know is as little as a grain of sand in a desert compared to all that we could learn. For that reason I always force myself to learning new things. At the beginning it can be really scary and overwhelming learning a new technology, language, skill, etc; but the sattisfaction that comes once we have mastered it is indescribable.
